Timmie Thumb and the Dark Forest
The Dark Forest lurks with friends and foes, thieves and martians...tames the monkeys and whispers to sapplings, raging its arms in swirls. Mom and Timmie were strolling in the Dark Forest to spend quality time. Enchanted by the pink worms in the loops of ground, Timmie sees his mom disappear, swallowed by the dark night.
Timmie needs to find his mom before the forest rests to black. Help Timmie find his mom!
